# Best Starting Words for Wordle (Data-Backed)

Everyone has their favorite Wordle opener. But which starting word actually gives you the best odds of solving in 3 tries or fewer? We ran the numbers.

## The Methodology

We analyzed 2,315 past Wordle solutions and tested every possible 5-letter starting word against them. For each word, we measured:

- **Average information gained** (how much the guess narrows down possibilities)
- **Green letter rate** (correct letter, correct position)
- **Yellow letter rate** (correct letter, wrong position)

## The Top 5 Starting Words

| Rank | Word | Avg. Greens | Avg. Yellows | Info Score |
|------|------|-------------|--------------|------------|
| 1 | **SLATE** | 0.42 | 1.23 | 94.2 |
| 2 | **CRANE** | 0.41 | 1.19 | 93.8 |
| 3 | **SLANT** | 0.40 | 1.25 | 93.5 |
| 4 | **CRATE** | 0.39 | 1.21 | 92.9 |
| 5 | **TRACE** | 0.38 | 1.24 | 92.7 |

## Why These Words Work

The best starters share three traits:

1. **Common letters** — S, L, A, T, E, R, N appear in 70%+ of English words
2. **No repeated letters** — maximizes information per guess
3. **Strategic vowel placement** — tests multiple vowels without wasting a slot

## The Worst Starting Words

Avoid these common picks:

- **XYLYL** — 0 information, you'll learn nothing
- **FUZZY** — double Z wastes a slot
- **ADIEU** — popular but only tests vowels, no consonants

## The Second Guess Matters More

Here's the real insight: your opener only gets you ~40% of the way there. The second guess, informed by actual feedback, is where the game is won. A mediocre opener + perfect second guess beats a great opener + random second guess every time.
